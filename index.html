SYPNOSIS A young man named Ryohei Arisu (Kento Yamazaki) is unemployed and has not had a very happy life. One day, a strange light appears, followed by. Except for Ryohei Arisu and his two friends, everyone vanishes. Tokyo is totally deserted. The lives of Ryohei Arisu and his two friends are at risk as they are compelled to participate in a survival game. Ryohei Arisu runs into Yuzuha Usagi there (Tao Tsuchiya).
MAIN CHARACTER The main character Arisu, a young gamer who is rejected by his own family because of his subpar performance in real life, struggles to find meaning in his waking hours.
OTHER CHARACTERS These are important characters that helps Arisu. They also play games to survive.
